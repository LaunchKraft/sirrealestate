How Sir Realtor Works

From your first search to keys in hand, Sir Realtor handles every step of your homebuying journey with AI-powered intelligence and care.

Find Your Perfect Home

Our AI never stops searching for you. Sir Realtor continuously scans listings and surfaces the best matches based on your criteria — going far beyond a simple keyword search.

  • AI Powered searches
  • Continual Agentic Searching
  • AI Powered Insights into neighborhoods, school districts, and best value

Book Viewings Effortlessly

Tell us your availability and Sir Realtor handles the rest. We'll contact the listing agent and coordinate viewing appointments on your behalf — no back-and-forth required.

  • Tell us your availability
  • We'll contact the listing agent on your behalf
  • Viewing appointments set up for you automatically

Make an Offer with Ease

When you're ready, Sir Realtor generates your offer via DropBox Sign and sends it to you and the seller(s)/seller's agent. Sir Realtor knows the right forms to use in all 50 states — you're in good hands.

  • Offer generated via DropBox Sign
  • Correct forms for all 50 states
  • We monitor offer status and keep you posted every step of the way

Close with Confidence

Sir Realtor walks you through every step of the closing process. For appointment-based steps, our AI uses calendar technology to schedule based on your availability. Each state has different forms and requirements — our AI continuously stays on top of them and tailors your experience accordingly.

  • AI calendar technology schedules closing appointments for you
  • State-specific forms and processes handled automatically
  • Continuously updated for each state's regulations

Sir Realtor will split the 3% commission with you, meaning you'll get 1.5% of your purchase back, just for using Sir Realtor.

For example on an $800,000 purchase, you would get $12,000 back.
